Privacy Policy  |  Effective Date: February 2026

Privacy
Policy.

Sidere AI, a specialist consultancy in neural architecture and cognitive pipelines, places the highest priority on data privacy. This policy outlines how we handle data collected via our website.

Information We Collect Automatically

When you visit the Sidere AI website, we collect minimal operational information essential for maintaining the integrity and security of the site. This may include standard web log data, such as IP addresses, browser types, and access times, aimed entirely at operational analytics rather than personal profiling. We utilize cookies purely for necessary administrative functions.

Your data is not our product. Your data is your intellectual property. Zero-Data Leakage starts at the front door.

Information You Provide to Us

If you choose to contact us via email (e.g., at solutions@sidereai.com), the personal information you supply (such as your name, corporate affiliation, and inquiries) is fundamentally treated as confidential business intelligence.

  • The information is exclusively utilized to respond to your specific inquiry and, if applicable, initiate a consulting engagement.
  • No information provided via the website is ever utilized to train internal or external neural network models without an explicit, formal, and separate data-sharing agreement.
  • We do not sell, rent, or distribute your contact information to third parties under any circumstances.

Client Data Engagements

Please note that this Privacy Policy pertains exclusively to interactions with the public-facing Sidere AI website. Engagements involving the engineering of cognitive infrastructures and the handling of proprietary corporate data are governed by rigorous, custom-drafted Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) and Master Services Agreements (MSAs). Our principle of Neural Sovereignty ensures that client data remains entirely cordoned from any external models.

Data Security

We employ enterprise-grade security protocols to protect the information you submit. However, given the nature of digital communications, no internet transmission is entirely secure. We encourage the use of encrypted communication channels for sharing sensitive operational data.

Contact Us regarding Privacy

Should you have any inquiries regarding your data or this policy, please direct correspondence to the Sidere AI Data Security Compliance unit at solutions@sidereai.com.
